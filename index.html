ENERGY STORAGE

Repurposing Orphaned Oil Wells - For Net‑Zero Emissions Power.

Matching generation to demand drives electricity prices, today gas peaking plants determine costs. Energy storage smooths out the imbalance, providing cheaper energy, and enabling increased use of Renewables.
Renewables now provide the lowest cost electricity generation across Canada.

Alberta has 193,976 wells for reclamation,
3,406 of which are orphaned
...and the best Class III onshore wind speeds.

Below we've mapped each orphaned well onto Windy's windspeed map.
Energy is stored when the wind is blowing, and released to meet peak demands in the evening.
When the wind blows the orphaned well fills with compressed air.
